Meet Ciara

Color is her potion. The macabre is her muse.

Ciara Fraiser Scott is a master colorist who turns hair into living stained glass — molten sunset melts, electric mermaid fades, and prismatic rainbows pulled straight from a midnight spell.

Her chair is a cauldron of creativity: gothic at heart, fearless with color, and obsessed with the haunting beauty of Halloween. Whether you crave a whisper of violet or a full spectrum transformation, Ciara brews the perfect shade for you — and only you.
